TUTORIAL
Blue is your target
Green is your enemies
Dont get caught
Kill target by moving in space of his

CONTROLS
Arrow keys for cardinal directions.
Page up, page down, home, end for diagonals
(they make a nice box on your keyboard, unless laptop, use your head to figure which is which))
F5/Refresh to restart
Stun guards by bumping into them
